# Overview

Avodex is a decentralized exchange offering:

- **Spot trading** across listed pairs (e.g. AVT/USDT).
- **Perpetuals / derivatives** trading.
- **AI-powered grid-trading bots** that automatically buy low and sell high across a price range.

Avodex runs on its **own in-house matching engine** (it is not built on a third-party exchange backend). The engine provides the order book, a trading ledger, and trade execution; a dedicated grid service orchestrates automated strategies on top of it.

## Highlights
- Self-custodial deposits via an on-chain deposit vault.
- A real central-limit order book matching engine.
- Automated grid strategies with configurable price range and grid count.
